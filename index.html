Mission Statement

Alliance Children Society serves families by providing healthy, safe, and secure play-based early childhood education environment in which young children can start to develop their social, physical, intellectual, communication, and education skills. Our Society welcome all families with young children to join our caring and loving community to explore our Society’s philosophy.

Philosophy

Alliance Children Society provides play-based early childhood education curriculum inspired with Reggio Emilia approach. Our Society’s goal is to prepare children the best for their academic path and to buildup children’s personalities to be become full of love, joy, patience kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self control. Our certified early childcare educators encourage children to explore and initiate learning through play, respect children’s ideas, challenge children with open ended questions, and scaffold children through their experiments.

Value and Beliefs 

Alliance Children Society values and believes in Christian philosophy to love, to care, and to serve young children of the communities. Our Society recognizes children are the gifts from God and are created in the image of God. Our Society believes that God created all children full of curiosity and imagination, and love to explore this wonderful world. Our Society provide early education programs in according with Alliance Children Society’s Fundamental Statement of Faith and Principles. Our early childhood education programs are offered within Christ-centered environment and are planned to equip children based on foundation of Christian principles in the Bible. With our Society’s Christ-centered values and beliefs, children in our early education program will experience God’s love daily through thanksgiving prayers, singing Christian songs, listening to Bible stories, and understanding Bible verses.

Our Staff

Alliance Children Society is directed and operated by dedicated, nourishing, and caring early childhood educators. Our caring early childhood educators are certified under B.C. authority of community care and assisted living act and are fully educated with the qualifications required by the Child Care Licensing Regulations.

Early Childhood Education Program

Alliance Children Society’s fundamental program curriculum is provided based on our Society’s philosophy, values and beliefs to help children excavate their wonderful personalities. Our play-based early childhood education program prepares children for their academic path and buildup their interest towards Language, Math, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Geography), Music, and Art.

Daily Education Routines

help children to experience the fun of learning.

1

Circle Time

Children will enjoy our daily circle-time with interesting stories and nursery rhymes, phonics and language literacy, songs and music movements, music rhythm and instruments, early numeracy game, scientific reasoning, world exploration, personal hygiene and human body, plants and environments, animals and habitat, and Bible stories with thanksgiving prayers and praising songs.

2

Arts and Crafts Time

Children can express themselves through the creative arts and craft activities.

3

Quiet Reading Time

During the quiet reading time, children can ponder and have a time to relax.

4

Play Time

Playtime is the most exciting moment for children to enjoin the free play time, fine moment indoor time, and gross movement outdoor play.
